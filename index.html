Workout 2/1

this is a workout that requires 2 days of hard work with one day off

Day 1

  • Bench-press 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell flies 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbell Skull Crushers 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Thrusters 4 sets x 10 reps (jog after each set)

Day 2

  • Dead-lifts 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Bent over rows 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Shoulder Press 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Barbell Curls 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbell lateral raises 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Concentrated Curls 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Hammers Burnout (do these until you cant do anymore)
  • Thrusters 4 sets x 10 reps (jog after each set)

Off day

  • This is your day off. Do nothing more strenuous than walking on this day
