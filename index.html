Trouble in Riverwind

  • Background

    • The district of Riverwind is a peaceful place founded by the once revered ranger Lady Riverwind.

  • Introduction

    • Create a foundation for the players in Riverwind. They should be ready to defend it at all cost by the time the conflict takes place.

  • Barbarian attack

    • One of the barbarian Tribes has managed to break through the boundry dividing Riverwind from Neversnow. Any attempt to send a raven to the Riverwatch tower seems to fail.

  • Quest

    • Defend Riverwind from the raiding barbarian tribe.

  • Conflict 2

    • As it turns out the barbarians that breached the boundry between Riverwind and Neversnow, were just a overzealous scouting tribe led by a much bigger tribe. Perhaps the sherriff sends the party to Eversnow in search of reinforcements.

  • Quest 2

    • The party will need to convince the dwarves of eversnow to aid in the conflict by helping them fend off the ice trolls in one of the lower tunnels. During the conflict, the entryway collapses and the group will need to find another way out. They will need to face several encounters with ice trolls before finding the surface.

  • Resolution

    • With the help of Eversnow, the town of Riverwind will be able to defend from the oncoming invasion. A priestess of Leira will grant the party a +1 boon of Leira, which will give the party a +1 to attack and damage with thier chosen weapon. By this time the party should be level 3.
