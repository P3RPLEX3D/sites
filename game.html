The Story Structure of DND

  • Setting

    • Where the story takes place. A simple idea of the land or maybe just a starting town. keep it simple.

  • A Protaganist

    • The characters of the story. the Protaganist. In a DND story the main protaganist would be the villian since heroes are reactive. However the hero of the story needs to have a solid background as well. A character with nothing to lose will do nothing to keep it safe.

  • Motivation

    • What does the protaganist need? What are their goals? Desires? A hero with no quest is no hero.

  • Conflict

    • This is a series of roadblocks that the protaganist will encounter along the way to completing his goal.

  • Conclusion

    • Having completed his task, defeating the villan, or collecting the magical relic, the protaganist will want to return home or at least to some version of normal. He can finally relax all of his problems being solved. Or are they?
